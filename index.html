About Wisconsin

"Wisconsin" comes from an anglicized French translation of the Algonquin "Meskowsin", which is theorized to relate to the Red Granite that the now "Wisconsin River" flows through.
(wikipedia.org)

Wisconsin is the 25th largest US State (by landmass) and the 20th most populous. It is located in the Northern part of the Midwest US. Two of the five Great Lakes, Michigan and Superior, border the state to the east and north respectively. Wisconsin is bordered by 4 states, Illinois to the south, Iowa to the southwest, Minnesota to the west, and Michigan to the north. Wisconsin is nicknamed "America's Dairyland" as it is one of America's leading dairy producers, known especially for its cheese.
(wikipedia.org)
