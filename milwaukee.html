Milwaukee, Wisconsin

Milwaukee is the largest City in Wisconsin, located on the shore of Lake Michigan in the southeastern part of the state. There are many things to do in Milwaukee, countless events are held throughout the year, ranging from massive music festivals like Summerfest, to smaller more local celebrations like Germanfest. The city has a relatively busy nightlife, with a bustling downtown scene featuring tons of restaurants and bars.
(visitmilwaukee.org)

City Info

Milwaukee is home to the first typewriter, created in 1869 by Christoper Latham Sholes, who also perfected the QWERTY keyboard layout
