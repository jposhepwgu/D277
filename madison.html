Madison, Wisconsin

Madison is the second-largest city in Wisconsin, as well as the capitol city. It is located on an isthmus between Lake Mendota and Lake Monona in the south-central region of the state. Madison is home to many museums, festivals, and sporting events. Madison is one of the most bike-friendly cities in the United States, with many residents biking all year round.
(visp.wisc.edu)
